ABOUT

With a distinguished career spanning over a decade in event planning and decoration, EventbyCredo is dedicated to transforming your event aspirations into impeccable reality. Our commitment to excellence ensures every detail, from exquisite floral arrangements to sophisticated lighting and elegant table settings, is meticulously curated. Trust us to deliver unparalleled service and create unforgettable experiences that exceed your expectations.

Meet the Owner

NWAIGWE EKENE CREDO

CEO & Founder

As a seasoned event planner and decorator, I bring a creative touch and meticulous attention to detail to every project. My journey in the event planning industry began over a decade ago, driven by a deep love for design and a desire to create unforgettable experiences.


At EventbyCredo, I specialize in transforming venues into breathtaking spaces that reflect my clients' unique styles and themes. Whether it's a wedding, corporate event, or private party, my goal is to deliver seamless and spectacular events that leave a lasting impression.


From concept to execution, I work closely with clients to understand their vision and bring it to life with elegance and flair. My expertise includes floral design, lighting, table settings, and all the intricate details that make an event truly special.


Let's work together to create an event that you'll cherish forever. I'm excited to bring your dreams to life!
